ITEM 1. BUSINESS

HSBC Holdings plc is one of the world's largest banking and financial services organizations, serving approximately 42 million customers worldwide through four global businesses: Wealth and Personal Banking, Commercial Banking, Global Banking and Markets, and Asset Management. Our shares are listed on the London, Hong Kong, New York, Paris and Bermuda stock exchanges.

As of December 31, 2023, we had total assets of $2.92 trillion, customer loans of $867 billion, and customer deposits of $1.58 trillion. We operate in 62 countries and territories across Europe, Asia, the Middle East and Africa, North America, and Latin America. Our network covers approximately 6,400 offices worldwide, including branches, subsidiaries, and associated companies.

For the year ended December 31, 2023, we reported revenue of $62.8 billion, profit before tax of $30.3 billion, and a return on equity of 14.2%. Our Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1) ratio was 14.8%, reflecting a strong capital position. We announced a share buyback of up to $2.0 billion and a second interim dividend of $0.21 per share.
