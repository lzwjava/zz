MANAGEMENT'S DISCUSSION AND ANALYSIS

Results of Operations — DBS Group Holdings Ltd

Total income for the year ended December 31, 2023 increased 22% to a record SGD 16.5 billion from SGD 13.5 billion in the prior year. Net interest income rose 28% to SGD 11.2 billion, driven by asset margin expansion and loan growth of 4%. Net interest margin improved 19 basis points to 2.12%, benefiting from the rising interest rate environment.

Non-interest income increased 10% to SGD 5.3 billion, with fee income growing 8% to SGD 3.2 billion, led by wealth management and cards. Trading income rose 19% to SGD 1.4 billion, reflecting stronger customer flow and market-making activities.

Total operating expenses were stable at SGD 6.8 billion, resulting in a cost-income ratio of 41.2%, an improvement from 43.6% in the prior year. Staff costs decreased 2% as productivity initiatives offset salary increments. The improvement in operating leverage contributed to a record profit before tax of SGD 9.7 billion, up 26% from the prior year.
