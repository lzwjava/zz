Note 2. Revenue Recognition

We derive revenue primarily from subscription services, professional services, and hardware sales. Subscription revenue includes fees for access to our cloud-based software platform, which is recognized ratably over the subscription term, typically one to three years. Professional services revenue includes implementation, training, and consulting services, which are recognized as the services are delivered. Hardware revenue is recognized upon delivery and acceptance by the customer.
