Executive Compensation Philosophy

Our executive compensation program is designed to attract, retain, and motivate talented executives who can drive our business strategy and create long-term stockholder value. The program emphasizes pay-for-performance, with a significant portion of total compensation tied to the achievement of financial and operational goals.
