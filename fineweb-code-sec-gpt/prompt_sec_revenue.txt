The Company's revenue for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2024 was $3.2 billion, compared to $2.8 billion in the prior year, representing growth of 14%. This increase was primarily driven by a 22% growth in subscription revenue, partially offset by a 5% decline in licensing revenue. Net income attributable to common shareholders was $487 million, or $2.43 per diluted share, compared to $412 million, or $2.06 per diluted share, in the prior year. The effective tax rate was 21.5%, compared to 22.3% in the prior year.
