Item 1A. Risk Factors
Investing in our securities involves a high degree of risk. We face risks related to our international operations, including exposure to foreign currency fluctuations, geopolitical tensions, and compliance with multiple regulatory frameworks. Our business depends on our ability to attract and retain skilled personnel, and competition for talent is intense. Additionally, we rely on third-party cloud infrastructure providers, and any disruption to their services could materially adversely affect our operations. Changes in data protection regulations, such as the GDPR and CCPA, may increase our compliance costs and limit our ability to collect and process personal data.
