Mutual Fund Operations and Performance

Our asset management division manages 147 mutual fund strategies with combined assets under management of $89.4 billion as of December 31, 2023. Equity funds accounted for 52% of AUM, fixed income 31%, and multi-asset 17%. Net inflows totaled $6.2 billion for the year, driven by strong performance in our emerging markets equity fund which returned 18.4% versus the benchmark 12.1%.

Management fee revenue increased 12% to $1.1 billion, reflecting average fee rates of 62 basis points. Distribution costs and trailer fees totaled $340 million. Operating expenses were flat at $420 million as technology investments offset headcount reductions.
